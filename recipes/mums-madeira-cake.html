Mum's Madeira Cake

Description

Don't underestimate this simple recipe - the cake packs a punch and is very moreish!

Ingredients

  • 175g margarine
  • 175g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 250g self-raising flour
  • 2-3 tbsp sherry

Method

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4. Grease an 18cm/7in round cake tin, line the base with greaseproof paper and grease the paper.
  2. Mix everything together in large bowl.
  3. Spoon the mixture into the prepared tin and lightly level the top. Bake on the middle shelf of the oven for 30-40 minutes, or until golden-brown on top and a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.
  4. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then turn it out on to a wire rack and leave to cool completely.
